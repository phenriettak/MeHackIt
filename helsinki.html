Hello Helsinki!

Our Helsinki workshop is taking place June 3-4th at Startup Sauna. During the days (10am-3pm), we'll have different stations where children tour in small groups, playing, painting and experimenting with electronics and technology. The day long events are organized by our amazing team!
The workshop is primarily meant for 6-12 year olds. We also have a café for parents who want to stick around!

Link to the webshop below! Limited amount of tickets available.
